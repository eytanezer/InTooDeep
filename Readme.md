# InTooDeep 🌊

**InTooDeep** is an atmospheric 2D puzzle-adventure game built in Unity. You play as the last surviving member of a doomed diving expedition. 

Your mission: descend into the unforgiving depths, navigate hostile waters guarded by relentless piranhas, gather scattered keys, and finally unlock the cursed treasure chest that claimed your crew.

---

## 🎮 Play the Game
Play directly in your browser on itch.io:

---

## ✨ Key Features
* **Atmospheric Narrative:** A slow-burn, melancholic story about survivor's guilt and completing a fatal mission.
* **Tense Exploration:** Navigate tight underwater corridors while dodging aggressive, dynamic piranha enemies.
* **Puzzle & Collection:** Scavenge the depths for hidden keys required to unlock the final chest.

---

## ⌨️ Controls
* **Movement:** `W A S D` or `Arrow Keys`
* **Interact:** `space` (or primary action button)


---

## 🛠️ Technical Overview
This project was developed for a university gaming lab. It showcases a decoupled, event-driven architecture to keep systems modular and clean.

**Core Technologies & Packages:**
* **Engine:** Unity 
* **Language:** C#
* **Camera System:** Unity Cinemachine (Virtual Cameras & Target Tracking)
